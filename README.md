CareLotto

CareLotto is a participatory social sculpture that transforms chance into collective care through transparent onchain funding, generative art, and community impact.

Overview

Every play contributes to three outcomes:

Support for a community organization
Support for a creative or social impact project
Entry into an individual prize pool

Participants receive a unique digital artwork and an onchain receipt documenting their contribution.

CareLotto explores how systems typically designed for extraction can be redesigned to generate care, connection, and shared value.

How It Works
Connect your wallet
Choose a cause or project
Purchase a CareLotto ticket
Receive a generative art receipt
Funds are transparently distributed onchain
Participate in a provably fair prize drawing
